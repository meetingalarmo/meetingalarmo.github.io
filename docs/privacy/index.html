Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 18, 2026

This policy explains how MeetingAlarmo (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the Timerval iOS app or these legal and support pages.

Timers stay local No account or server sync is used for timer configurations.
No ad tracking No advertising SDK or Firebase Analytics is included.
Limited diagnostics Crash reports help us find and fix reliability problems.

Information we handle

Timer and app data stored on your device

Timerval stores the configurations you create, including names, timer modes, durations or BPM values, tone frequencies, ordering, chaining choices, and app state such as the currently selected configuration. This information is stored in the app’s local container using Apple-provided storage.

We do not operate an account, cloud-sync, or backup server for this data, and the app does not send this timer content to us. Your Apple device backup settings may include the app’s local container in a device or iCloud backup managed by Apple.

Crash and reliability diagnostics

Timerval uses Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google, to receive crash reports and categorized non-fatal errors. Crashlytics may process crash stack traces, relevant app state, device and operating-system information, app version and build, Crashlytics or Firebase installation identifiers, and diagnostic transport metadata.

We add only bounded reliability context: whether a timer was running, whether it was finite or used BPM intervals, interval count, categorized stop and timer-failure reasons, and categorized purchase stage or failure reasons. We intentionally do not attach timer names, durations, frequencies, product identifiers, email addresses, user identifiers, or free-form messages to these reports.

The diagnostic data is used to maintain app functionality and reliability. It is not linked by us to your identity and is not used to track you across apps or websites. Firebase Analytics is not included.

Purchases

Apple processes Timerval Pro subscriptions and lifetime purchases. The app requests localized product information and receives cryptographically verified transaction and entitlement status through StoreKit so it can unlock Pro features. We do not receive or store your payment-card details. Apple handles purchase data under its own privacy terms.

Support and contact data

If you email us, we receive the email address, message, and any information or attachments you choose to provide. You do not need to provide timer content or payment information to request support.

These web pages

These pages do not use cookies, advertising, analytics, forms, or client-side scripts. They are hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process information such as IP addresses and request logs to deliver and secure the hosting service under GitHub’s own privacy terms.

How we use and share information

We use information only as reasonably necessary to:

  • provide purchases and app functionality;
  • diagnose crashes, prevent data loss, and improve reliability;
  • reply to support, privacy, and legal requests; and
  • comply with law and protect users, our rights, and the service.

We share information with service providers only for those purposes, principally Google for Crashlytics, Apple for App Store and StoreKit services, GitHub for page hosting, and email providers for messages you send. We do not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may disclose information if required by law or as part of a business transfer, subject to applicable safeguards.

Retention

  • On-device timer data: remains until you edit or delete it, reset the app, or uninstall Timerval, subject to any Apple-managed device backups.
  • Crash diagnostics: Firebase states that Crashlytics retains crash stack traces and associated identifiers for 90 days before beginning removal from live and backup systems.
  • Support email: kept only as long as reasonably needed to resolve the request, maintain necessary business records, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.
  • Purchase records: retained by Apple according to Apple’s policies. The app keeps only a local cached entitlement indicator for faster startup and rechecks it with StoreKit.

Your choices and rights

  • You can edit or delete saved configurations in the app.
  • You can remove local app data by uninstalling Timerval and manage Apple backups through your device settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions and review purchases through your Apple Account settings.
  • Crashlytics collection is automatic while the configured app is installed; Timerval currently does not offer an in-app diagnostic collection switch.
  • You may ask us to access, correct, or delete support information we hold, or object to or restrict its processing, where applicable law provides those rights.

We may need enough information to verify and complete a privacy request. You may also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

International processing

Our service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Where required, they and we rely on appropriate legal mechanisms and safeguards for international transfers.

Children

Timerval does not require an account and is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child sent us personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app, data practices, service providers, or legal requirements change. We will post the revised policy at this same URL and change the date above. Material changes will receive additional notice when required.
